Who i am

I am Roger Haus living in Barcelona. When I was young, brushes and paint were never leaving my pockets. I learnt to draw at the same table where Picasso and Miró created their universes. Today, I am on front page of Opensea with my Exquisite Workers project. I draw fun comics, chat stickers and design for the United Nations.
